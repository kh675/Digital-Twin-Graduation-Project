=============================================================================
VISUALIZATION COMPARISON
=============================================================================

t-SNE:
- Focuses on local neighborhoods
- Good for identifying clusters
- Slower for large datasets
- Non-deterministic (even with random_state)

UMAP:
- Preserves global structure better
- Faster computation
- More consistent results
- Better for understanding overall data distribution

3D PCA:
- Linear dimensionality reduction
- Fastest computation
- Interpretable components
- Shows explained variance
